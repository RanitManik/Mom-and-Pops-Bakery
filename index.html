About us

Mom & Pop's Bakery, est. 2010, is a cozy nook of sweetness nestled in the heart of Kolkata. We're a family-owned venture, with Grandma's secret apple pie recipe as our guiding star.

Every morning, our team of bakers hand-selects the ripest fruits and the richest chocolates. We take pride in creating treats that taste like home, with a dash of whimsy thrown in for good measure.

Whether it's a dainty cupcake or an elaborate wedding cake, each creation is a labor of love. Drop by for a slice of comfort and a cup of camaraderie. At Mom & Pop's, every visit is a cherished memory in the making.
